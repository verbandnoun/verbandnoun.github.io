Results

The business wanted more enterprise retailers to pay their vendors early and they didn't know why so many weren't taking action on such great opportunities for early payment. We knew people were using the product but, they weren't taking advantage of the early payment opportunities in the product.

C2FO is a tool used by corporate accounts payable teams as an alternative to their typical investments for cash they have on hand. "Get a great return on your cash by paying your vendors early, strengthen your supply chain."


These users were immersed in an office environment.


In their role, they were reporting up and managing down and were judged by the clarity and prudence of their decision-making in regards to financials.


They were likely desktop users and their likely tools were Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook.

Business challenge

Background & Context

The business wanted enterprise users to act on more of their accounts payable

This client was focusing on their aftermarket strategy at the time of this project. A key part of that strategy was improving the effectiveness of their dealerships in planning and acting on machine alerts. And in that they wanted to create a new, fuller product out of one that already existed as a tab within a separate system.


The key business goals were…

Allow machine monitoring specialists to find issues more quickly.

Speed up time to machine pre-diagnosis.

Consolidate the information going to service locations.


Each alert represented an opportunity to service a customers machine, thereby generating revenue.
