I’ve had a decent stint of time at both startups and enterprises alike. They’re both distinct environments where internal culture can differ quite drastically. A start up can be small and nimble in how it adjusts to market conditions; communication is easy, simple and coherant. Albeit a lot of times, the startup isn’t sure where exactly its going. So, communcation becomes about making sure objectives are made clear.

Designing in this kind of environment is highly rewarding. The team cohesion and communication is fluid. And the ideas that churn out are not mired by enterprise baggage or policy.
